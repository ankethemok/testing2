About Me

I am a student majoring in Global Business and Digital Arts at the University of Waterloo. Creating things that are both functional and pleasing to the eye is the foundation of my designs. I am passionate about exploring the psychology behind design, how I can curate my designs to be the most captivating. At my most recent job, I was a Social Media Manager and Photographer for EastRidge Church, where I built a lot of skills in video editing and social media marketing. I’m interested in bringing my design skills into brand design.
